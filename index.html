Ahmed Morsi

Ahmed Morsi is an Egyptian artist based in New York city. An art critic and poet with a career that spans seven decades of creative output. In the 1950s, he simultaneously studied literature at Alexandria University and painting at the studio of Silvio Becchi, the son of Italian master Otorino Becchi.

Ahmed Morsi's Different Art Eras

Alexandria 1947-1956

Baghdad 1956-1957

Cairo 1958-1974

New York 1974-Present

"...The atelier remains, as though I had not abandoned it, for more than 60 years, embracing the sea view. The unfamiliar Royal Ras Al Teen Palace, moorings of the shipping boats, screams of the seagulls..."

-Ahmed Morsi, "Elegy for the Mediterranean Sea".
